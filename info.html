About ChannelBot

Channelbot was made to make servers more fun, and at the same time, simplify channel permissions. This bot has a lot of channel modes, channel features, and more to encourage users to chat!

ChannelBot simplifies the process of creating and managing channels in your servers with fast permissions, server channel setup, and other fun features. Just forget about spending time creating your server, and do it all in one command. This bot includes speedy permissions, channel setup, channel modes, fun, moderation, and more!
