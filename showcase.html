Why I Use ChannelBot!

Hey I'm from Fortnite Nobility (15k+members). We use channel bot to easily lock down the channel so only moderators can type in the main chat! It works so easily and it's obvious to everyone that the channel has been locked with the nice little lock animation! When the channel is locked we can get our message across and do the countdown. Again, the bot saves so much time, and without the bot, it takes a while to dive into channel settings!
